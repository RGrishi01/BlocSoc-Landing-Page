What's it all about ?

This event will bring together talented developers, blockchain enthusiasts, and industry professionals from around the world to collaborate, create, and innovate in the blockchain space. With a focus on real-world applications of blockchain technology, participants will have the opportunity to showcase their skills, learn from experts in the field, and compete for prizes.

We'd love to see something related to whatever esoteric technology you might have a weird obsession with. So, pick a technology you love and start gritting day and night to build something on it. Or build something entirely new on your own!
